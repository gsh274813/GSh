About the Shop

Located in the heart of Baltana, Gupta Sanitary & Hardware Store has built a strong reputation for delivering premium quality sanitary and paint products. With decades of service to our name, we’ve become a household name in the region for homeowners, contractors, and interior decorators.

We pride ourselves on offering a wide assortment of branded items including top-of-the-line sanitary ware and paint solutions. Our team is dedicated to helping every customer find the right products for their project with personal service and expert advice.

Mr.Puneet Gupta, the visionary founder of Gupta Sanitary & Hardware Store, has been a cornerstone of trust and quality in the sanitaryware and hardware industry for over 25 years. With a career built on unwavering dedication, product authenticity, and personalized customer service, he has become a respected figure among homeowners, contractors, and architects alike throughout Punjab.
